Cultural Diversity

Elementsia's culture is a rich tapestry woven from the threads of diverse traditions, languages, and customs brought together by its people. The nation's central location has made it a crossroads for trade and exchange, fostering a fusion of ideas and a celebration of diversity.

The Cultural Diversity Festival, held annually, showcases the vibrant array of Elementsian traditions, from dance performances to culinary delights.

Religion

Most Elementsians are Christsian, Protestant. Many however are also catholic. Christianity is the only allowed religion in the RFE as the local culture fully supports it. The Temple of Serenity, nestled in the tranquil countryside, serves as a place of meditation and reflection for many.

Architecture

Elementsian architecture is a unique blend of various styles, drawing inspiration from South African, American, European, and tropical island designs. Over time, the architecture evolved to accommodate the rapid growth of cities, leading to a fusion of forms that reflect the nation's diverse influences.

Many urban centers in Elementsia adopted a high-density Japanese architectural style, creating a distinctive urban landscape characterized by towering skyscrapers and efficient land use.
