Lavon Puckingtun

The girl who grew up in a small town outside of Belkburg. She didn't have much, but made work with what she had. She went into politics and from there she married into the monarchy.

A popular book in the RFE titled: Lavon's Love for the People, tells her story, truly inspiring. She made education more accessible to those who didn't have the opportunity.

King Militar

This man was a militarist. He came to power through a democratic election, although barely. He is quite often known for a city named after him, known as Militar. His goal was to rule the continent on which Elementsia lies in order to be self-sufficient. With the idea of a perfect and controlled environment, that didn't succeed, but he put up a good fight for his dream.

His legacy continues with Batil wanting the same. Some love him and admire his work, while others despise him and his plans.
