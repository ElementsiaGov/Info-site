Timeline of Elementsian History

  • 362BC - Group of people gather and decide to become independent
  • 257BC - Gets recognized as a small town
  • 113BC - Becomes a fully autonomous region
  • 276AC - Gets invaded by nearby superpower
  • 279AC - Gathers last supplies and people to settle off to a new place
  • 300AC - Arrives on the continent of Lamplight
  • 316AC - Finds Tertën (The island on the Westcoast of the RFE)
  • 526AC - Totally mapped out Tertën
  • 678AC - Trades with more outside nations
  • 789AC - Monarchy gets established
  • 792AC - Democracy comes into play
  • 865AC - Militar is born
  • 896AC - Militar gets elected head of state
  • 934AC - Militar assassination
  • 934AC - Lavon Is born
  • 957AC - Lavon forced into office
  • 1005AC - Lavon dies
  • 1489AC - Kartof Establishes colony near Harbor Bay
  • 1520AC - De Orianne and Genova establish base near Vertin
  • 1615AC - First civil war
  • 1620AC - Both foreign countries get wiped off the continent due to Elementsian hostility
  • 1746AC - Trade agreements
  • 1834AC - Constitution gets established (Modern one)
  • 1924AC - Communist rise
  • 1926AC - Elementsia suppresses citizens brutally as well as communist getting executed
  • 1978AC - Hendroff born
  • 2005AC - Hendroff as president and monarch
  • 2024AC - Hendroff resignation and Elizabeth death
  • 2024AC - André Stockhilm as president
  • 2024AC - André Stockhilm resigned, replaced by Hendroff
  • 2024AC - New constitution
  • 2024AC - Hendroff back as head of state
