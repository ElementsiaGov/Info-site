Elementsia's First Civil War

The rebel group of Kalmar had risen up against the government, seeking to overthrow the existing regime and establish a new order. Initially, the government had hoped to placate the rebels with concessions and promises, but it soon became clear that the Kalmar rebellion was driven by an unshakable desire for freedom and democracy.
