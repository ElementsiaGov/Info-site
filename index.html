The Kingdom of Elementsia-RFE-

The Kingdom of Elementsia-RFE- is a massive, cultured nation, ruled by Hendroff Puckingtun with an iron fist, and remarkable for its free-roaming dinosaurs, otherworldly petting zoo, and parental licensing program. The compassionate, cynical, devout population of 1.648 billion Elementsians are ruled by a mostly-benevolent dictator, who grants the populace the freedom to live their own lives but watches carefully for anyone to slip up.

The medium-sized government juggles the competing demands of Law & Order, Education, and Defense. It meets to discuss matters of state in the capital city of Harbor Bay | Kimberlen. The average income tax rate is 59.2%, and even higher for the wealthy.

The all-consuming Elementsia-RFE-ian economy, worth 171 trillion Kimhars a year, is broadly diversified and led by the Beef-Based Agriculture industry, with major contributions from Tourism, Information Technology, and Uranium Mining. Average income is an impressive 103,955 Kimhars, and distributed extremely evenly, with little difference between the richest and poorest citizens.

The rich tend their gardens inside gated urban farming communities, trails to natural wonders are littered with trash from reality film crews, the government is hoping that medical aid will speak louder than human rights abuses, and a shipwreck is everyone's fault but the captain's. Crime, especially youth-related, is totally unknown, thanks to a well-funded police force and progressive social policies in education and welfare. Elementsia-RFE-'s national animal is the Tiger, which frolics freely in the nation's many lush forests, and its national religion is Christianity.
