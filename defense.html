Defense Vehicles

All-Terrain Personnel Carrier

Approximately 754 of these carriers are in service or can deploy. The carrier is fitted with rear, side, and top cameras ensuring a 360° view of its surroundings. It is an 8×8 beast with a .50 caliber M2 turret on top that can spin 180°.

It has a total capacity of 15 personnel, counting the driver and gunner: 17.

T15 Tank

This tank is super expensive but heavily armored and armed. It has a 105mm gun alongside heat seeking capabilities. The tracks of this monster are absolutely unstoppable. With massive wheels, it can even cross shallow waters.

It has an auto reload feature. About 179 are in service.

It houses 3 personnel:

  • Heat seeker
  • Gunner
  • Driver

Armored Engineering Vehicle (AEV)

This vehicle is often deployed on repair missions as it is its intended duty. It is armored as well. The vehicle has a turret with a .25 caliber gun on top. It is usually deployed to more developed regions with roads and rails since it can't handle hills well.

About 378 are in service.
